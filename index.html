How It Works

Players take turns drawing cards from three difficulty levels - SPARK, GLOW, or SHINE. Each card poses a question or challenge that invites authentic sharing. Complete the card, and the higher the difficulty level you choose, the more threads you earn.

Throughout the game, you'll have Appreciation Tokens to give to others whenever their sharing touches you. While threads track the game's progress, the real victory is in the connections you create.

Three Levels of Connection

SPARK Cards

Quick connections that brighten the moment. Perfect for breaking the ice and creating instant warmth in any conversation.

GLOW Cards

Moderate challenges that reveal new colors. Balance imagination with introspection - invent stories, share meaningful memories, or see yourself through new lenses. Where playfulness meets meaning.

SHINE Cards

Complex challenges that push boundaries. Ready to fully engage - mentally, creatively, and emotionally. From improvised performances to vulnerable truths to creative collaboration.
